COURSES OFFERED

at Footwear Academy

Welcome to our shoemaking courses! We're passionate about sharing our expertise with you and helping you develop the skills needed to create your own beautiful shoes.

We offer formalized footwear training programs that can be assessed online & offline. We are building the largest community of shoemakers in Africa and connecting trained shoemakers to job matching, collaboration and investment opportunities.
