The Footwear Academy

Welcome to The Footwear Academy. At The Footwear Academy, we equip young people with a core focus on women, to produce export standard leatherworks products. Our mission is to reduce poverty, unemployment, and foster socio-economic development in Nigeria. Our ambition for Africa is to equip indigenous leatherworks brands with the necessary skills that will enable them to compete globally.
