About The Footwear Academy (TFA)

We Train & Connect Africa Shoemakers to The World

We started “The Footwear Academy’ in 2017“ with the sole purpose of making leatherworks easy, affordable and accessible to young people with a core focus on women and people living with disabilities.

We are building the largest community of shoemakers in Africa and connecting trained shoemakers to job matching, collaboration and investment opportunities.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to reduce poverty, unemployment and foster socio-economic development. We are also at the forefront of sustainable footwear manufacturing in Africa

Our ambition for Africa is to equip indigenous footwear brands with the necessary skills that will enable them to compete globally.home

We offer both online and offline courses, so you can choose the learning style that suits you best.

No matter which course you choose, you'll receive personalized attention and support from our instructors every step of the way. We believe that everyone has the potential to become a skilled shoemaker, and we're committed to helping you achieve your goals.
