Shoe Making

Welcome to a world where your passion for footwear takes shape, one stitch at a time. The Footwear Academy invites you to embark on a journey of creativity, craftsmanship, and innovation through our comprehensive shoe making courses.

We offer both online and offline courses, so you can choose the learning style that suits you best

Our offline courses, provide you with the opportunity to learn in a hands-on, immersive environment. You'll work closely with our experienced instructors to create your own shoes from start to finish.

Our courses cover a wide range of topics, from basic shoe design and pattern-making to advanced construction techniques.

Our online courses,on the other hand are designed to be flexible and convenient, allowing you to learn at your own pace and in the comfort of your own home. You'll have access to a wealth of instructional videos, tutorials, and resources that will guide you through the entire shoemaking process.

Why Choose Shoe Making with Us?
  • Our seasoned shoemakers and industry experts lead the way. Learn from the best as they guide you through the intricate process of creating beautiful and functional footwear.
  • Whether you're a beginner or an experienced artisan, our courses cater to all skill levels. Choose from a variety of programs, from basic shoe construction to advanced design techniques.
  • Unleash your creativity. Craft shoes that reflect your unique style. Our courses empower you to design and create footwear tailored to your vision.

COURSE PATH

BASIC class lasts one month and covers slippers and sandals making

INTERMEDIATE class lasts two months and covers slippers making, sandal making and oxford shoemaking

MASTER class lasts four months and covers slippers making, sandal making, Oxford, Derby, Loafers and Decollete shoemaking
